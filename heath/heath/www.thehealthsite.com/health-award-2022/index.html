About The Award

The Healthcare Ecosystem is a combination of providers (hospitals, doctors, therapists, and others), payers (health insurers), suppliers (pharmaceutical and MedTech companies, pharmacies, and others), and regulators.

 

Zee Digital brings to you the first edition of Health Awards 2022 to acknowledge and recognize the movers, leaders, and achievers in the health sector of India. Each contributor will be rewarded based on a set of parameters – efforts and innovations.

 

The Zee Health Awards 2022 will be an amalgamation of turnaround discussion on how Indian Healthcare ecosystem needs to evolve and equip itself post pandemic and awarding the saviours and leaders in Healthcare Ecosystem.
